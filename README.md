**ChatNest** is a powerful, privacy-first browser extension designed to organize and save your AI conversations. It adds **folders, tags, and instant search** to your favorite AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini — directly inside their sidebars.

## 🚀 Why ChatNest?

If you use AI assistants daily, your sidebar quickly becomes a mess of hundreds of unnamed conversations. ChatNest solves this by giving you the organizational tools missing from the native interfaces.

- **📁 ChatGPT Folders:** Organize your chats by project, client, or topic.
- **🏷️ Flexible Tagging:** Add multiple tags to any conversation for cross-folder organization.
- **🔍 Global Search:** Find any saved AI chat in milliseconds across all supported platforms.
- **🔒 100% Private:** Your data stays in your browser. We never see your conversations.

## 🌐 Supported Platforms

One extension to rule them all. ChatNest works seamlessly inside:
- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **Gemini** (Google)
- **Perplexity**
- **Copilot** (Microsoft)
- **Grok** (xAI)
- **DeepSeek**

## ✨ Key Features

### Organize Like a Pro
Don't just scroll — manage. Create nested folders and use tags to build a personal knowledge base from your AI interactions.

### Instant Productivity
Stop switching between tabs or apps to find that one specific prompt or answer. Our integrated search works right where you chat.

### Multi-Platform Sync (UI)
The same organization structure follows you across all 7 supported AI assistants, providing a unified experience.

## 🛠️ Installation

1. Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/bphdbonieamdappjndenhelenebmjdib).
2. Click **Add to Chrome**.
3. Open any supported AI (like chat.openai.com) and look for the ChatNest folder icon in the sidebar.
